Using the Web App: Student Management System
1. Accessing the Web App :

➡️ Open a web browser (Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, etc.).
➡️ Enter the URL of the web app in the address bar.
➡️ Press Enter to load the web app.

2. Homepage :

➡️ The homepage provides an overview of the student management system.
➡️ It may contain information about the purpose of the system, its features, and navigation options.

3. Teacher Registration :

➡️ By Default The Username and Password for teacher login is "admin" and "admin" respectively.
➡️ If you want to make a new Teacher account then redirect to /teacher_register from the above browser navbar.
➡️ Click on the "Register" button to create your account.
➡️ If the chosen username is already taken, you will be prompted to choose a different one.

4. Teacher Login :

➡️ After registration, return to the homepage.
➡️ Click on the "Teacher Login" link.
➡️ Enter your registered username and password.
➡️ Click on the "Login" button.
➡️ If the entered credentials are correct, you will be redirected to the teacher dashboard.

5. Teacher Dashboard :

➡️ The teacher dashboard provides an overview of student data and options for managing student records.
➡️ It displays the total number of registered students and a list of their usernames.
➡️ Click on a student's username to access their details and marks.

6. Mark Editing :

➡️ In the student details page, you can edit the marks for each subject.
➡️ Enter the numeric marks in the respective input fields.
➡️ Once the marks are entered, click on the "Save" or "Update" button to save the changes.
➡️ The updated marks will be stored in the system's database.

7. Student Registration :

➡️ If you are a student and don't have an account, click on the "Student Registration" link on the homepage.
➡️ Fill in the required fields, including a unique username and a secure password.
➡️ Click on the "Register" button to create your account.
➡️ If the chosen username is already taken, you will be prompted to choose a different one.

8. Student Login :

➡️ After registration, return to the homepage.
➡️ Click on the "Student Login" link.
➡️ Enter your registered username and password.
➡️ Click on the "Login" button.
➡️ If the entered credentials are correct, you will be redirected to the student dashboard.

9. Student Dashboard :

➡️ The student dashboard provides a personalized view of your marks.
➡️ It displays your username and the marks obtained in different subjects.
➡️ You can review your performance and track your progress over time.

10. Additional Pages :

➡️ The web app may contain additional pages such as "Contact" and "About".
➡️ The "Contact" page uses "FIREBASE" provides information on how to get in touch with the system administrators or support team.
➡️ The "About" page gives a brief overview of the project, including its purpose and goals.

11. Logging Out :

➡️ To log out, click on the "Logout" button located in the navigation menu or on the respective dashboard page.
➡️ Logging out will end your session and redirect you to the homepage.

12. Further Development :

➡️ The web app may feature a "Coming Soon" page, indicating future enhancements and additional functionalities that are planned for implementation.

By following these steps, teachers can register, log in, manage student records, and update marks. Students can register, log in, view their marks, and track their progress. The web app simplifies the process of student management and provides an organized platform for teachers and students to interact effectively.
