studentMATE : Modern Student Management System

The online student management system is a web-based platform designed to streamline the management of student records and marks. It caters primarily to teachers, who can register and log in to access the teacher dashboard. This dashboard serves as a central hub for managing student data, displaying the total number of registered students and providing a list of all students. Teachers can view individual student details, including their username and marks in different subjects.

The system simplifies the process of updating student marks. By clicking on a student's name or ID, teachers can access the mark editing page for that student. Here, they can easily enter the marks for various subjects, ensuring accurate record-keeping. The updated marks are saved in the database.

Students have their own login functionality and can register for an account. Once logged in, they can access their personalized dashboard. The student dashboard displays their username and marks obtained in different subjects, allowing them to track their performance and progress.

To enhance the user experience, the system includes additional pages such as contact and about pages. The contact page provides information on how to reach system administrators or the support team, while the about page gives an overview of the project's purpose and goals. These pages provide relevant information and foster transparency. Additionally, a "Coming Soon" page indicates planned future features, highlighting the system's continuous development.

In summary, the online student management system simplifies the management of student records and marks. It enables efficient communication between teachers and students, offers real-time access to important information, and promotes accurate record-keeping. By leveraging web technologies and user-friendly interfaces, the system aims to enhance the educational experience and contribute to a more organized and effective learning environment.

Problem Statement & Solution
Problem Statement :

Create an online student management system that allows teachers to log in, manage student records, and update their marks. Students should be able to log in, view their marks, and register for an account if they don't have one.

Solution :

The implemented solution is an online student management system that utilizes Flask, SQLite, and HTML templates. It offers various routes to handle different functionalities, such as teacher and student login, registration, dashboard views, mark editing, and basic website pages.

Teachers can log in to access the teacher dashboard, where they can manage student records and update their marks. Students have their own login functionality, allowing them to view their marks and register for an account if they don't already have one.

The system provides a user-friendly interface using HTML templates, ensuring easy navigation and interaction for both teachers and students. It leverages Flask to handle routing and server-side logic, while SQLite serves as the database for storing student and teacher information.

Overall, this solution addresses the problem of managing student records and marks by providing an efficient and accessible online platform. Teachers and students can easily access the system, view relevant information, and perform necessary actions, thereby enhancing the management of student data and improving the educational experience.

Features of the Online Student Management System:
Teacher Registration and Login : Teachers can register with a unique username and password. They can log in to access the teacher dashboard.

Teacher Dashboard: Teachers can view the list of students and the total number of registered students. They can navigate to the mark editing page for each student to update their marks.

Mark Editing: Teachers can edit the marks of each student individually by accessing the mark editing page. They can enter numeric marks for different subjects and save the changes.

Student Registration and Login: Students can register with a unique username and password. They can log in to access their individual dashboard.

Student Dashboard: Students can view their marks for different subjects. The dashboard displays their username and the marks obtained in each subject.

Contact and About Pages: The system provides additional static pages such as contact information and an about page to provide more details about the project.

Coming Soon Page: There is a placeholder page indicating that more features or functionalities are coming soon.
